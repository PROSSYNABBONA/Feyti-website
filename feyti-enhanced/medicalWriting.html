Medical Writing & Scientific Communication

In the complex realm of medical and scientific communication, where precision and clarity are paramount, Feyti Medical Group emerges as your beacon of excellence. We recognize the profound impact effective communication holds in advancing groundbreaking research, shaping informed healthcare decisions, and empowering individuals worldwide.

Medical Writing

We write, edit, and format various types of documents, such as Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), manuscripts, abstracts, posters, slides, clinical trial study protocols, study reports, reviews, newsletters, brochures, and consumer drug/medical device information. We also provide literature search, data analysis, and reference management services.

Scientific Editing

We proofread and revise your documents to ensure they are clear, concise, accurate, and consistent. We check and correct grammar, spelling, punctuation, and style. We also assist with formatting, referencing, and aligning documents with submission guidelines for your target journals or publishers.

Translation

We translate documents from and into various languages, including English, Kiswahili, and French. We also offer proofreading services to ensure your translated content maintains accuracy and quality across languages.
